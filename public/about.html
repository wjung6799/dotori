About Dotori

Dotori(도토리)—Korean for acorn—is a K–5 learning center where curiosity takes root and literacy grows. Through rich content and guided instruction, we build strong literacy skills, background knowledge, and critical thinking. We also offer Korean classes and fun, one-day workshops for all ages.

About Yesol

Hi, I’m Yesol Jung—an educator, curriculum nerd, and the heart behind Dotori School. I started this little school with a big dream: to create a space where language, identity, and joyful learning come together. I’ve been teaching in public schools for almost a decade, working in more than 20 different schools across Washington State. From Title 1 schools to high-performing districts like Lake Washington and Bellevue, I’ve had the privilege of learning alongside students from all walks of life. I hold Washington State teaching certifications with endorsements in Elementary Education, English Language Learners (ELL), and Korean Language, and I’m proud to be a National Board Certified Teacher. I’ve also taught Korean language and culture at the United Seattle Bellevue Korean School, where I started as a teaching assistant and later became a lead teacher and mentor. During my six years teaching 1st and 2nd graders, my focus was on helping heritage learners and beginners fall in love with the Korean language through content-based lessons that blend culture, creativity, and connection. When I’m not teaching, you can usually find me doing creative writing—poetry, short stories, and sometimes just scribbling ideas in a notebook. I also love CrossFit and weightlifting; I believe in staying strong in both body and mind. One fun fact about me: I was the only girl on my high school varsity wrestling team! Dotori School may still be small, but it’s rooted in years of classroom experience, a love of language, and a belief that education should be both rigorous and full of heart. Thank you in advance for the opportunity to support your child(ren)’s learning—I’m so excited to grow alongside them on their educational journey.
