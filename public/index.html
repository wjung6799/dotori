About Dotori

Dotori (도토리)—Korean for “acorn”—is where curiosity takes root and learning flourishes.
We welcome learners into a warm, creative space filled with opportunities to grow in English reading and writing, as well as explore Korean language and culture. While our Reading and Writing programs are taught fully in English, our Korean classes offer engaging bilingual programs designed for both heritage and non-heritage learners.
