Frequently Asked Questions

What payment options are available?
We offer flexible payment options including full payment upfront (with 10% discount), monthly installments, and income share agreements for qualified students.
Do I need prior experience to enroll?
Most of our programs are designed for beginners, though some advanced courses may require basic knowledge. Each program page lists specific prerequisites.
What kind of support do you provide during the program?
You'll have access to instructors, teaching assistants, peer study groups, office hours, and our student success team throughout your journey.
Are the programs available online or in-person?
We offer flexible learning formats including fully online, in-person, and hybrid options. You can choose what works best for your schedule.
What happens after I complete a program?
You'll receive a certificate of completion, lifetime access to our alumni network, ongoing career support, and access to advanced courses and workshops.
